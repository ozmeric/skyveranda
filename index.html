The Technology

One remote. Total control of your outdoor space.

Every Pergolette pergola is built around a motorized louvered roof — aluminum blades that rotate up to 170° at the touch of a button, so your patio adapts to whatever the day brings.

01

Rotating Louvers

Turn the roof panels from fully open to fully closed in seconds — full sun, full shade, or anywhere in between.

02

Weather Sensors

Wind and rain sensors close the louvers automatically the moment conditions change, even when you're not home.

03

Integrated Lighting

Built-in LED strips along the frame light up your evenings, from quiet dinners to backyard gatherings.

04

Retractable Screens

Drop-down side screens add privacy and keep bugs out, retracting again with a single button when you don't need them.

How It Works

From first call to first dinner outside.

Most residential installs go from consultation to finished pergola in under three weeks.

BLADE 01

Consult

We visit your property, measure the space, and talk through how you actually want to use it — dining, lounging, hosting, or all three.

BLADE 02

Design

You'll see a rendering matched to your home before anything is ordered, and choose your frame color, louver finish, and add-ons.

BLADE 03

Install

Our crew handles footings, framing, and wiring on site. Most residential pergolas are fully installed in two to three days.

BLADE 04

Enjoy

We walk you through the remote and app before we leave, so you're opening and closing your roof the same day.
